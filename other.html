Marden High School Rules: Other



        Other rules that the school has:

        • Chewing gum is banned in school, so students are not allowed to bring it into the school grounds.
          If they are caught with some, they will be asked to put it in the bin.
        • Bikes should not be ridden down the pavement leading up to the school. This includes riding a bike, or standing on the pedal.
        • Students are not allowed to swing on chairs, as this is dangerous.
