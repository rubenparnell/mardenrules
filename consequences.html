Marden High School Rules: Consequences



Unacceptable behavior can lead to consequences (as stated in The Student Handbook, page 7).

Classroom Sanctions:

  • C1: Verbal warning,
  • C2: Second verbal warning,
  • C3: Classroom sanction,
  • C4,
  • C5

Detentions:

Due to CoViD-19, Detentions have been changed.

  • Detentions will be heald the day after a student had recieved the detention.
  • Detentions will last for 15 minutes, from 3:15 to 3:30.
  • Detentions will be held with the student's head of year.
  • These detentions can be for any reason, incuding behavior, lateness, and homework.
  • The member of staff who teaches the student period 5 will walk them to their head of year for their detention.
  • The time spent will be restorative, discussing 1:1 how best to avoid similar sanctions in future.
  • A text message will go out to parents / carers on the day the detention is set so they are aware of the detention the following day.


Detentions can be given for many reasons, including:

  • Being repeatedly late to school or lessons,
  • Not following the school's expectations,
  • Not completing homework on time (the class teacher should give the student up to 24 hours to get the homework to them. If this is not completed, a detention will be issued).
  • Not wearing the correct school uniform,
  • Repeatedly not having the correct equipment, if the teacher thinks this is neccecary,
  • Being generally silly in lessons or at break/ lunch times,
  • Breaking the science lab rules/ other rules set out by a teacher,
  • And many more.
